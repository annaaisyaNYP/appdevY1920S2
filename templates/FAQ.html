Payments


You can purchase items using Visa and MasterCard.

Your orders and payments are visible in the order history.

You will be sent a confirmation email upon successful placement of order.

Orders


  • Credit/Debit card payments: refund will take between 7- 14 working days
  • Bank transfer payments: refunded to shopee wallet within 24 hours

Only one voucher is allowed in a single checkout

You may cancel your order by tapping on Cancel Order in Order Detail page

Deliveries

  • For overseas shipping: $6 - $13

  • Maximum weight: 30kg per package

  • For overseas shipping: within 9-24 days
